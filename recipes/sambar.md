# Sambar

## Regional Classification
Tamil Nadu

## Dietary Tags
Vegetarian, Gluten-Free

## Ingredients
- 1 cup toor dal
- 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
- 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
- 1 teaspoon urad dal
- 10 curry leaves
- 2 dried red chilies
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 2 tablespoons sambar powder
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (drumstick, carrot, brinjal)
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil
- Salt to taste

## Substitutions Applied
None

## Steps
1. Wash and pressure cook toor dal with 2 cups water until soft
2. Soak tamarind in warm water and extract pulp
3. Heat coconut oil in a heavy-bottomed pan
4. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter
5. Add urad dal and fry until golden
6. Add curry leaves and dried red chilies, sauté for 30 seconds
7. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender
8. Add tamarind extract, turmeric powder, and sambar powder
9. Add cooked dal and simmer for 10 minutes
10. Season with salt and serve hot with rice or idli
