# Medu Vada

## Regional Classification
Tamil Nadu

## Dietary Tags
Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-Free

## Ingredients
- 1 cup chana dal
- 1/4 cup rice flour
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1 teaspoon black pepper, coarsely crushed
- 10 curry leaves, finely chopped
- 2 green chilies, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, finely grated
- 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups coconut oil (for deep frying)

## Substitutions Applied
- Urad dal → Chana dal (ratio: 1:1)

## Steps
1. Wash and soak 1 cup chana dal in water for 4 hours
2. Drain the dal and grind to a thick, fluffy batter using minimal water (1-2 tablespoons at a time)
3. Add 1/4 cup rice flour, 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, 1 teaspoon crushed black pepper, chopped curry leaves, green chilies, grated ginger, asafoetida, and salt to the batter
4. Mix well and whip the batter by hand for 2 minutes to incorporate air
5. Heat 2 cups coconut oil in a deep pan to 170°C (340°F)
6. Wet your hands, take a lemon-sized ball of batter, shape into a round disc, and poke a hole in the center with your thumb
7. Gently slide the vada into the hot oil
8. Fry for 2 minutes on one side until golden, then flip and fry for another 1-2 minutes
9. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels
10. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar
